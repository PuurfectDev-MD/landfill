## What is landfill?

Landfill is my personal portfolio — a place to store and cherish the projects I've worked on over the years. It's where people can discover my work, uncover my maker life and hopefully get a little inspired (let's just hope you are).

No perfection here. Its just me with real projects, real mess, real making.

---

## Inspiration

Landfill was inspired by [Candance's scrapyard website](https://sagecadence.framer.website/about), which I stumbled upon on Instagram. A cool alternative to "scrapyard" was **landfill** — and it fit exactly what I was going for. A place to dump my projects :)

## Built with

- **HTML** — structure
- **CSS / Tailwind** — styling
- **Vanilla JavaScript** — interactivity
- **GSAP** — animations
- **Vercel** — hosting (via GitHub repo)

Building it was genuinely fun — especially the GSAP animations, the home page, and the `.been` page. Go check those out if you haven't already.
